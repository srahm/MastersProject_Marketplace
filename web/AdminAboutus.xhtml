What is MyMarketPlace?

MyMarketplace is a fully functional website where students and faculty at Northeastern Illinois University can sell their products and/or services. This site provides students and faculty the ability to create an account, upload pictures with a brief description, and view posted items. The intent of this site is to help you sell or purchase items at a discounted price without having to extensively search the Internet. Simply email the seller to arrange a time and place to meet. To avoid clutter and unwanted solicitation, students and faculty members with an neiu.edu email address can only access the marketplace and posts older than a month will be removed. Good Luck!
